We are a non-profit global humanitarian organization with focus on inspiring and engaging communities to help serve humanitarian causes. We have been providing free healthcare, food, education, health and fitness related services, and housing assistance to the needy individuals and families

5000+ blood donations

3000+ bonemarrow screening
